About Us

Welcome to Katapathar Resort, a beautiful hotel located in Uttrakhand. Our resort is surrounded by stunning nature and offers a peaceful escape for everyone. Whether you're looking for a cozy room or a luxurious suite, we have comfortable accommodations with great views. Our restaurants serve delicious food made from fresh ingredients, and you can enjoy activities like hiking, water sports, and spa treatments.Whether you're here for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a business event, we have everything you need for a memorable stay. Our friendly and attentive staff is committed to providing personalized service and creating a welcoming atmosphere for all our guests. Experience genuine hospitality and discover the magic of Katapathar Resort, your ultimate destination for luxury and relaxation.
