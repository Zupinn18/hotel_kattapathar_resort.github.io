FACILITIES

Welcome to our hotel katapathar resort, where we pride ourselves on providing top-notch facilities to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable stay for our guests. From the moment you arrive, you'll be greeted by our friendly staff and a welcoming atmosphere that sets the tone for a memorable experience. Our accommodations are designed with your comfort in mind, offering spacious rooms or suites. Whether you're traveling solo, with your family, or for business, we have the perfect room to suit your needs. When it comes to dining, our hotel boasts an array of culinary options to tantalize your taste buds. Enjoy a sumptuous buffet breakfast to kickstart your day, indulge in delicious meals at our onsite restaurant featuring a diverse menu of local and international cuisine. For recreation and relaxation, our hotel offers a range of facilities to keep you entertained. Take a refreshing dip in our sparkling pool, or simply unwind in our steam rooms. Families will love our dedicated children's play area and activities to keep the little ones engaged. In addition to these facilities, we also provide concierge services, airport transfers, and 24-hour room service to cater to your every need during your stay. Whether you're visiting for leisure or business, our hotel is committed to providing an exceptional experience with top-notch facilities and personalized service.
